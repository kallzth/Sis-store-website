Shipping Information
Free delivery on orders over ETB 500For orders under ETB 500, a flat fee of ETB 50–100 applies depending on your location.
How ordering works
All orders are placed via WhatsApp. Once you send your order from our store, our team confirms availability, shares payment details and arranges delivery — all within a few hours.
Same-day dispatch
Orders confirmed and paid before 2:00 PM on business days are dispatched the same day. Orders after 2 PM or on weekends are dispatched the next business day.
Order tracking
Once dispatched, we send a WhatsApp update. For Addis Ababa our delivery person contacts you directly. For other cities a courier tracking number is provided.

Return Policy
7-day return windowYou have 7 days from the date of delivery to request a return.
✅ Items we accept back
Items must be unused and unwashed, in original condition with all tags still attached, returned in original or similar packaging.
❌ Items we cannot accept back
The following cannot be returned:
  • Sale or discounted items (marked SALE)
  • Items that have been worn, washed or altered
  • Items without original tags
  • Underwear or intimate apparel
  • Items returned after 7 days
How to return an item
1
Message us on WhatsApp
Contact us at +251 982 602 264 within 7 days. Tell us your name, the item and the reason for returning.
2
Send a photo
Send a clear photo of the item showing its current condition. This helps us process your return quickly.
3
We arrange collection
Our team will collect the item from your location. For Addis Ababa, collection is free. For other cities you may need to drop it at a courier office.
4
Refund or exchange processed
Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund or exchange is processed within 2–3 business days.
Size Exchanges
Free size exchange
Message us within 7 days of delivery. Tell us your order and the size you need. If available, we send the replacement and collect the original at no extra cost.
If your size isn't available
We'll offer you a full refund or store credit to use on another product — your choice.
Refunds
Refund timeline
Refunds are processed within 2–3 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
Refund methods
Refunds are sent via your original payment method — Telebirr, CBE Birr or bank transfer. Cash refunds available for cash-on-delivery orders.
Original delivery fee is non-refundableUnless the return is due to our error (wrong or damaged item).
Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged item or the wrong product, we'll make it right immediately — no questions asked.

1
Don't use the item
Keep it as received with all original packaging and tags if possible.
2
Contact us within 48 hours
WhatsApp us at +251 982 602 264 within 48 hours of delivery with a clear photo of the issue.
3
We fix it immediately
We dispatch a replacement or issue a full refund — your choice — at no cost to you.

Our team responds quickly — usually within a few hours on WhatsApp.
