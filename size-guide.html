Shoe Sizes
Measure your foot at the end of the day when feet are at their largest. Stand on a flat surface for accuracy.
📏
How to measure
Place your foot on paper, mark your longest toe and heel, then measure the distance between the marks.
⏰
Measure in the evening
Feet swell slightly during the day — measuring in the evening gives a more accurate size.
🧦
Wear your usual socks
Measure while wearing the socks you'll normally wear with your new shoes.

Kids' Clothing Sizes
Kids' sizes are based on age and height. We recommend measuring height for the most accurate fit as children vary greatly in size.
📏
Height
Stand straight against a wall. Mark the top of the head and measure from the floor.
📐
Chest
Measure around the fullest part of the chest, keeping the tape comfortable and level.
📏
Waist
Measure around the natural waistline while relaxed — do not pull the tape tight.

Free size exchanges
If your size doesn't fit, we offer free size exchanges within 7 days. No stress — we've got you covered.

Measure twice
Always measure twice for accuracy. Use a flexible tape measure and measure at the widest point of each area.

Fit varies by style
Slim-fit styles run smaller. Oversized or relaxed styles may run larger. Check the product description for specific fit notes.
