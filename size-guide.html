Men's Fit Tips
📏
Between two sizes?
Go up a size for a relaxed fit, or stay at the smaller size for a slim/fitted look.
👕
Shirts & Jackets
Shoulder seams should sit at the edge of your shoulder — this is the most important fit point.
👖
Jeans & Trousers
Waist is priority — you can always hem the length, but a tight waist is uncomfortable all day.
🔄

How to Measure Yourself
📏
Bust
Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
〰️
Waist
Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waistline, usually above the belly button.
🔄
Hips
Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat, about 20 cm below your waist.
📐
Dress Length
Measure from the highest point of your shoulder straight down to where you want the hem.

Women's Fit Tips
👗
Dresses & Blouses
When bust and waist suggest different sizes, size up and use a belt or tuck to define the waist.
👖
Trousers & Skirts
Go by the largest measurement (waist or hips). A slightly loose waistband is easy to adjust.
📐
Fitted vs Relaxed
For fitted styles size to your exact measurements. For relaxed/oversized styles go down a size.
🔄
Free exchanges
Not 100% sure? Order — if it doesn't fit we'll swap it for the right size within 7 days, free.
How to Measure Your Foot
📏
Foot Length
Stand on paper, trace your foot and measure from heel to longest toe in cm. Do both feet and use the larger measurement.
🕐
Measure in the Evening
Feet swell throughout the day. Measure in the afternoon or evening for the most accurate size.
🧦
Wear Your Socks
Measure while wearing the type of socks you'll wear with the shoes for an accurate reading.
🔄
Between Sizes?
Always go up half a size. Shoes that are slightly long are more comfortable than shoes that are tight.

How to Measure Your Child
📏
Height
Have your child stand straight against a wall. Measure from the floor to the top of the head.
〰️
Chest
Measure around the fullest part of the chest, just under the armpits, keeping tape horizontal.
🔄
Waist
Measure around the natural waistline — where the child normally wears their trousers.
📐
Room to Grow
Kids grow fast! For longer wear, size up — especially for outerwear and formal wear.
