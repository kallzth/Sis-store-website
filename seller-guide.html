Step by Step
How to Start Selling in 4 Simple Steps
From registration to your first sale — here's exactly what happens and what to expect at each stage.
1
Register Your Shop
Fill in the Seller Registration form with your personal details, shop name, the categories you sell in and a password for your dashboard. The form takes about 2 minutes.
Choose a memorable shop name — customers will see it on every product you list.
2
Get Approved (within 24 hours)
Our team reviews your application and contacts you via WhatsApp within 24 hours. Once approved, your account is activated and you can log into your Seller Dashboard immediately.
Make sure your WhatsApp number is correct — that's how we contact you for approval and orders.
3
Add Your Products
Log into your Seller Dashboard and add your products directly. Upload photos, set prices, choose categories, add sizes and stock quantities. Your products go live on the store as soon as you save them.
Products with clear photos and detailed descriptions get 3× more orders. Use natural light for product photos.
4
Receive Orders on WhatsApp
When a customer places an order, their full details — name, items, sizes, quantities — are sent directly to your WhatsApp number. You confirm, arrange payment and handle delivery directly with the customer.
Respond to orders within 2 hours for the best customer experience and repeat buyers.

FAQ
Common Seller Questions
Everything you need to know before getting started. Still have questions? WhatsApp us directly.
Ask on WhatsApp
Is it really free to join?
Yes — completely free. No registration fee, no monthly subscription, no listing fees. We only charge a small commission on completed sales, which is agreed with you during onboarding.
How do customers pay me?
Customers pay you directly via Telebirr, CBE Birr, bank transfer or cash on delivery. You handle payment and delivery with the customer through WhatsApp. We never hold your money.
How many products can I list?
No limit! List as many products as you have. Start with your best sellers and expand over time. Adding more products = more chances to make sales.
Can I update my products anytime?
Yes! Log into your Seller Dashboard anytime to add, edit or remove products. Change prices, update photos, adjust stock — changes go live immediately on the store.
What if I receive a bad order?
You have full control. You can confirm or decline any order through WhatsApp. If a customer has an issue, you handle returns and exchanges directly. Our admin team is available to help resolve any disputes.
Do I need to handle delivery?
Yes — you arrange delivery directly with the customer. You can use your own delivery contacts, courier services, or arrange for customer pickup. This gives you full control over delivery costs and timing.
